Ahalia KEAM Model Exam 2021

may 18 2021

The following points regarding Ahalia KEAM Model Exam were discussed today. Please suggest any changes Ahalia KEAM online model exam can be held either on 4th or 11th April 2021 as KEAM exam schedule is third week of April probably on 18th April 2021. Results for KEAM Model Exam can be declared either on 6th or 13th April 2021. Website www.ahaliaccp.com can be used to register and conduct the exam. Last date of registration for the model exam can be 2nd April 2021. Facebook of Ahalia school of engineering and technology can be used for promoting Ahalia KEAM Model Exam 2021. For maximum reach among the students and parents, the registrations can begin from March 2nd 2021 . The web page will be ready for registration from 2nd March 2021 onwards. It can be called as Ahalia KEAM Online Application Processing System. Posters for Ahalia KEAM Model Exam can be circulated from March 2nd 2021 onwards as the effectiveness would increase with increase in the time the poster is available for circulation. Format for the question paper will be sent on 2nd march 2021. Trail run can be held on 15th March and 22nd March.
